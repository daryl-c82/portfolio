# About Daryl Chan
## Professional Profile

Daryl Chan is a Digital Transformation Strategist, AI Product Builder and Founder of **Journey Inspired (J. Inspired)**, a Malaysian platform dedicated to curating transformational travel experiences anchored in health and wellness tourism, connecting travellers to education, property, eco and cultural experiences across Malaysia.

With more than 12 years of experience leading enterprise initiatives across insurance, banking, hospitality and tourism, he specialises in digital transformation, product strategy, project portfolio management and AI-enabled business innovation.

## Journey Inspired

Journey Inspired was established to rethink how travellers discover meaningful experiences.

Rather than operating as a conventional travel agency, Journey Inspired is being developed as a digital platform that connects travellers with trusted healthcare providers, wellness partners, educational institutions, tourism operators and lifestyle experiences throughout Malaysia.

The platform combines technology, trusted partnerships and personalised concierge services to create journeys that contribute to personal wellbeing, lifelong learning and long-term value.
