# AI Projects

> Applying artificial intelligence to solve practical business challenges.

## Overview

Artificial Intelligence is becoming a core capability across industries.

Rather than viewing AI as a standalone technology, I approach it as an enabler of better decision-making, automation and customer experiences.

My work focuses on applying AI responsibly to real-world business challenges across tourism, digital transformation, project management and knowledge management.

## AI Philosophy

AI should augment human expertise rather than replace it.

My approach prioritises responsible adoption, practical implementation and measurable business outcomes over technology for its own sake.
