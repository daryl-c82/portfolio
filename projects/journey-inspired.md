## Overview

Journey Inspired (J. Inspired) is an AI-powered travel concierge platform focused on helping travellers discover trusted transformational experiences across Malaysia.

The platform is anchored in health & wellness tourism, with education, property, eco and cultural experiences strengthening the ecosystem around it.

## The Challenge

Travellers planning specialised journeys often need to coordinate multiple providers across different industries.

Information is fragmented, planning is time-consuming and trust can be difficult to establish.

## My Approach

Rather than creating another online travel agency, I am building a platform that connects travellers with verified partners through curated recommendations and AI-assisted planning.

The emphasis is on trust, personalisation and long-term relationships.
